Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

ElimuSaaS is a school management platform built for Kenyan schools. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

  • Student information (names, admission numbers, class, grades, attendance)
  • Parent/guardian contact details (phone numbers for SMS alerts)
  • Staff information (names, TSC numbers, employment details, payroll)
  • Fee payment records and financial transactions
  • Login credentials (passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text)
  • Usage logs for security and performance monitoring

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Providing school management services (exams, fees, attendance, communication)
  • Sending SMS/WhatsApp notifications to parents about their children
  • Generating reports and analytics for school administrators
  • Processing M-Pesa payments via Safaricom's API
  • Improving platform features and fixing bugs

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on secure servers hosted on Render.com with PostgreSQL databases. We implement industry-standard security measures including JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing, HTTPS encryption, and role-based access control.

Each school's data is completely isolated — School A cannot access School B's data under any circumstances.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. We only share data with:

  • Safaricom (for M-Pesa payment processing)
  • SMS service providers (for parent notifications only)
  • Render.com (our hosting provider, under a data processing agreement)

6. Your Rights

School administrators can request data export, correction, or deletion by contacting us. Students and parents can request access to their personal data held by the school.

7. Data Retention

School data is retained for the duration of the subscription plus 12 months. After that, data is permanently deleted unless the school requests an export.

8. Cookies

ElimuSaaS uses localStorage (not cookies) to store session tokens on your device. No tracking cookies are used.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify school administrators by email of any significant changes to this policy.
