🔒 Privacy Policy

How ElimuSaaS collects, uses, and protects the personal information of schools, staff, students and parents

📋 Summary for School Administrators ElimuSaaS is a school management platform. We process data on behalf of schools (as a data processor) under their instructions. Schools are the data controllers for their students and staff data. We do not sell any personal data and do not display advertisements.

🏢 1. Who We Are

ElimuSaaS Ltd ("ElimuSaaS", "we", "us", or "our") is a technology company registered in Kenya, providing school management software-as-a-service (SaaS) to educational institutions across Kenya and East Africa.

Our registered address: Nairobi, Kenya. Contact: privacy@elimusaas.com

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The ElimuSaaS web platform (elimu-saas-frontend.onrender.com)
  • The ElimuSaaS backend API (elimu-saas.onrender.com)
  • The ElimuSaaS Android application
  • Any related services, tools, or communications

📊 2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Schools Provide

When a school registers or uses ElimuSaaS, they provide:

  • School information: Name, physical address, county, phone, email, KNEC code, school type
  • Administrator details: Full name, email address, phone number, role
  • Staff records: Names, employee IDs, TSC numbers, email addresses, subjects taught, class assignments, salary grades
  • Student records: Full names, admission numbers, dates of birth, gender, class/stream, KCPE index numbers, medical information where provided
  • Parent/guardian details: Names, phone numbers, email addresses, relationship to student
  • Academic data: Exam marks, grades, attendance records, teacher remarks, CBC assessment levels
  • Financial data: Fee payment amounts, M-Pesa receipt numbers, bank transfer references, fee balances

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Login timestamps and IP addresses (for security)
  • Device type and operating system (mobile app analytics)
  • Pages visited and features used (usage analytics, aggregated)
  • Error logs for debugging (no personal content)

2.3 Payment Information

ElimuSaaS integrates with M-Pesa (Safaricom Daraja API) for fee collection. We store M-Pesa receipt numbers and transaction amounts for record-keeping. We do not store full M-Pesa PINs or card numbers.

🤝 4. Data Sharing

We share personal data only in the following circumstances:

  • Within a school: Staff can only see data for their own school. Role-based access controls ensure teachers see only their classes, bursars see only financial data, etc.
  • Service providers: We use trusted sub-processors to operate the platform:
    • Render.com — Cloud hosting (servers located in the EU/US; covered by standard contractual clauses)
    • Safaricom M-Pesa — Mobile payment processing
    • Africa's Talking — SMS delivery
    • Neon (PostgreSQL) — Database hosting
  • Legal requirements: If required by Kenyan law, a court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, data would transfer to the new entity under the same privacy protections
🔒 School Isolation School A can never access School B's data. Every API request is validated against the authenticated school's tenant ID. Our multi-tenant architecture enforces strict data isolation at the database level.

🛡️ 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: Database encrypted at rest on Render/Neon infrastructure
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with short expiry; bcrypt password hashing (cost factor 10+)
  • Access controls: Role-based access control (RBAC) — 8 distinct roles with scoped permissions
  • Rate limiting: API rate limiting to prevent brute-force attacks
  • Android security: Certificate pinning, root/emulator detection, ProGuard obfuscation
  • Audit logs: All admin actions are logged with timestamps and user IDs
  • Dependency scanning: Regular security updates for all dependencies

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your school, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware.

👶 7. Children's Privacy

ElimuSaaS processes data about students, including minors under 18. This processing is done:

  • Under the authority of the school (as the data controller)
  • For legitimate educational purposes only
  • With the school acting as a trusted intermediary between us and the students/parents

Schools are responsible for obtaining any required parental consent for students under 18 before enrolling them in ElimuSaaS. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children without school authorisation.

Student data is never used for advertising, profiling, or shared with commercial third parties.

✅ 8. Your Rights

Under Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you or your students
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data (school admins can do this directly in the system)
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to data portability: Export your school's data in CSV/PDF format
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to restriction: Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@elimusaas.com. We will respond within 21 days.

If you believe your rights have been violated, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner of Kenya.

🍪 9. Cookies & Local Storage

ElimuSaaS uses minimal cookies and browser storage:

  • Session token: Stored in memory/localStorage to keep you logged in
  • Theme preference: Light/dark mode setting
  • Offline cache: Recent data cached locally in the Android app for offline use

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. No cookie consent banner is required as we only use strictly necessary cookies.
