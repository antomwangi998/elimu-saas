About ElimuSaaS

ElimuSaaS is a Kenyan-built school management platform designed to solve the real problems faced by Kenyan schools — from fee collection to CBC assessment, timetabling to payroll. We understand the local context: M-Pesa integration, KNEC requirements, CBC learning areas, and the day-to-day needs of Kenyan school administrators.

Built on modern technology, ElimuSaaS is fast, secure, and accessible from any device — including Android phones. Our platform supports both 8-4-4 and CBC curricula, making it suitable for all levels from Primary through Secondary school.
