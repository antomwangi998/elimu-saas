ElimuSaaS Documentation

Welcome to the ElimuSaaS documentation. This guide covers all features of the platform.

Overview

ElimuSaaS is a complete school management platform built for Kenyan schools. It supports both 8-4-4 (secondary) and CBC (primary) curricula, and integrates with M-Pesa for fee collection.

Logging In

Visit elimu-saas-frontend.onrender.com. Enter your email, password, and optional school code. The system will route you to the correct portal based on your role.

Fee Management

ElimuSaaS supports M-Pesa STK Push, Paybill (522522), bank transfer, and manual cash entry. Fee structures can be set per class, with automatic invoice generation and parent SMS alerts.

Setting Up M-Pesa

Go to School Settings → Integrations → M-Pesa. Enter your Safaricom Paybill/Till number and Consumer Key/Secret from the Safaricom Developer Portal.

Exams & Marks

Create exam series (term exams, CATs, mocks). Teachers enter marks for only their assigned subjects. The system auto-calculates grades, mean marks, and positions using the configured grading scale.

CBC Curriculum (Primary)

CBC assessments use a 4-level scale: EE (Exceeding Expectation, 80-100%), ME (Meeting Expectation, 50-79%), AE (Approaching Expectation, 30-49%), BE (Below Expectation, 0-29%). Learning areas, strands, and sub-strands are configured per grade level.

8-4-4 Curriculum (Secondary)

Form 1 students take 11 subjects (including additional electives). Forms 2–4 take 8 subjects. Grades follow the standard KNEC scale: A (75-100, 12 pts) through E (0-24, 1 pt). Mean grade is calculated from the best subject scores per KCSE rules.

Timetable

The auto-generator creates conflict-free timetables ensuring no teacher is in two classes simultaneously. To seed sample data, go to Timetable → click "Seed Sample Data".

Attendance

Daily attendance is marked per class. Parents receive SMS if their child is absent. Monthly attendance reports are auto-generated.

Payroll & HR

Staff payroll with automatic PAYE, NHIF, and NSSF deductions. Generate payslips and P9 forms. Supports TSC-registered and non-TSC staff.

Communication

Broadcast SMS to parents, teachers, or all staff. WhatsApp integration for individual messages. Newsletter module for bulk email-style announcements.

Subscription & Billing

Subscriptions are billed per term. Pay via M-Pesa Paybill (522522), STK Push, or bank transfer (Equity Bank, A/C: 0180293648191). Contact us to renew or upgrade.
