HOW IT WORKS
Up and running in 10 minutes
1

Register Your School

Contact us or sign up online. We onboard your school in minutes with your school code and admin credentials.

2

Import Your Data

Upload your student list via CSV or add them one by one. Set up classes, subjects, and fee structures.

3

Install the App

Open in Chrome on your phone, tap "Add to Home Screen". Works like a native app on any Android or iPhone.

4

Invite Your Staff

Add teachers, HODs, and bursars with their email addresses. They each get their own secure login.
